The Brick Bot

The Brick Bot is the robot used in the FIRST Lego League robot game by the Brick Bot Builders (60419). The Brick Bot was originally chosen in a competition during the 2023-24 Masterpiece season, and has recieved updates and iterations as the season progressed. Some examples of iterations to the robot include: a clasp to secure the back of the robot and ease in changing attachments, stickers to inform about the front and back of the robot as well as the program numbers, and more!
