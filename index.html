What is FLL?

FLL, or First Lego League, is a youth robotics competition under the organization FIRST. First Lego League consists of three levels, which span form ages 4-16. This competition teaches how the core values of teamwork, inclusion, innovation, discovery, impact, and most importantly, fun.

The Problem and Our Solution

Throughout the world, researchers, scientists, hobbyists, and others strive to explore and learn about the ocean. However, for many, the costs of purchasing or building equipment is far too high or difficult. Our solution merges and takes inspiration from underwater remotely operated vehicles (ROVs) and camera traps on land, leading to a cost-effective solution that is almost 3x-1000x cheaper than a standard ROV.

Our Robot

The FIRST Lego League robot game competition is a 2:30 match in which teams try to score as many points as possible by an autonomous robot. The Brick Bot, used for this part of the competition, features many iterations to both the robot itself and the attachments it uses.
