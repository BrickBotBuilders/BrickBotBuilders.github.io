What is FLL?

Water management is the process of moving water so that it doesn't harm humans, property, or nature. This form of management also makes sure that the water runs efficiently and gets where it is going.

The Problem

Across the global stage, there is a plentiful amount of water that will last us for generatioins, yet some areas have excess water and many have inadequate supplies of it.
